Help: How to play

How to win:

  • Select your character and defeat all other characters before losing all of your HP(health points).
  • Do this with all four characters to advance to the next level.
  • Each level has their own set of characters and environments. (There are 3 Level at this time)

Player Area Controls:

  • When the game starts, choose a character by clicking on the fighter's picture. You will fight as that character for the rest of the game.
  • You must then defeat all of the remaining fighters.
  • You choose an opponent by clicking on an enemy's picture.
  • Once You select an opponent, your character and your enemy are moved to the battlefield.

Battlefield Controls:

  • You will now be able to click the attack or retreat button.
  • Whenever you click attack, your character damages the defender. The opponent will lose HP(health points). These points are displayed at the bottom of the defender's picture.
  • The opponent character will instantly counter the attack. When that happens, your character will lose some of their HP. These points are shown at the bottom of your character's picture.
  • You have the option to click retreat at any time to leave the battlefield and choose a different opponent.
  • The battle will continue until either your character or your opponent is drained of all of their HPor you retreat.
  • When you defeat an opponent, close the battlefield and choose your next opponent.
  • After you defeat all opponents, you will continue the level by choosing a new character to fight with.
  • Once you have won the game with all four characters, you can advance to the next level.

Hints:

  • Each character in the game has 3 attributes: Health Points, Attack Power and Counter Attack Power.
  • Each time your character attacks, their Attack Power increases by its base Attack Power.
    • For example, if the base Attack Power is 6, each attack will increase the Attack Power by 6 (12, 18, 24, 30 and so on).
  • The enemy character only has Counter Attack Power.
    • Unlike your Attack Points, Counter Attack Power never changes.
    • A winning player must pick their characters wisely.
